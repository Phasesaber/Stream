What is Stream?

Stream is a Plugin creation service for Minecraft Servers. If you want a plugin, we can make it!* Once Sponge is officially out, we will offer support for Sponge plugins.

Who am I?

I'm Jadon "Phase" Fowler, a programmer, game designer, and fellow Minecrafter! I've been coding for years, and I know many languages including Java, Python, PHP, and more. You can find more information about me on my website.

What are Plugins?

Want to add some cool modifications to your server? Plugins can enhance your server's game play and customise things that usually can't be done in vanilla.
I offer a wide arrange of plugin choices, including Minigames, MySQL, Packets, NMS, and much more. Once Sponge is officially out, I will be offering Sponge plugins as well.
