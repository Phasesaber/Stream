Experience

Here are some links to plugins and mods we've made before:


JurassiCraft: A Forge mod that brings great dinosaurs to life!


PlayerPets: Who wants a cow? Now you can Players follow you around! With custom skins and names, the fun never stops!


Disco Blocks: Fancy color-changing blocks! Simply plop down where you want the LSD-blocks and watch your buildings come to life!


Game Client: A Minecraft client mod for all your minigaming needs! This adds buttons to popular servers to the home screen and inhances the in-game HUD.


Custom HD Skins & Capes: This client mod allows you to have custom HD capes and skins! Simply put the URL of any skin or cape in a .txt file and you're good to go!
